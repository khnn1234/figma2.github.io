Radial Measure

Radius Reach enables users to specify a geographical area for sharing content, such as videos, PDFs, and images, to maximize targeted engagement.

  • Easily adjust the sharing radius with a simple slider or input box.
  • Supports various content formats for versatile sharing.
  • Helps connect with a more relevant and engaged audience.
  • Provides a real-time preview of the selected area
  • Gives users control over who can view their content based on location.

Scannable Logo

Scan Share transforms content sharing by allowing users to upload and manage content through the Kenna logo on their apparel. Others can scan these logos to view or request access to the content.

  • Users upload content (videos, PDFs, etc.) to their profile by scanning the Kenna logo on their shirts.
  • The uploaded content is displayed on the user's profile feed and accessible through scans of the logo by others
  • Users can set content as private, requiring approval or a PIN for access.
  • Enhances social interaction by facilitating content sharing and access through physical logo scans.
  • Promotes engagement and brand interaction with the innovative use of scannable logos.

Feeds

Scan Share allows users to upload content by scanning Kenna logos and categorizing it into sport, connection, creative, workspace, or culture. Each category features its own like button, and users can share their feeds on social media.

  • Content Upload: Scan Kenna logos on apparel to upload videos, PDFs, images, and more to your profile feed.
  • Category Engagement: Each content category has a dedicated like button for more personalized interaction.
  • Social Sharing: Extend your reach by sharing your Scan Share feed on various social media platforms.

In App Real Messaging

Instant Chat is an in-app real-time messaging feature allowing users to send texts, voice notes, emojis, and feeds. It enhances communication by integrating various multimedia options for a dynamic messaging experience.
